Julianna said, "You should do it, because you get up first, and then we don't have to wait as long to get our coffee."

To which Mickey replied, "You are in charge of cooking around here and you should do it, because that is your job, and I can just wait for my coffee."

Julianna (in a higher pitch and louder voice) said, "That is my JOB?  No, no, no! You should do it, and besides, it is in the Bible that the man should do the coffee."

With a look of confused doubt and a smirk on his face, he says, "I can't believe that, show me."
